没有你做不到,只有你想不到

Let f = min(Li/Si), where
i ∈ {top, right, bottom, left},
Si is the sum of the two corresponding radii of the corners on side i, and
Ltop = Lbottom = the width of the box, and
Lleft = Lright = the height of the box.
If f < 1, then all corner radii are reduced by multiplying them by f.
